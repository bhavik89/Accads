Project Limitations & Future work

The project site has following limitations:

  • As the site uses only Google API, the site only returns the results given by the google, which may sometimes return limited results.
  • Reviews and photos of the place are limited the results returned by Google
  • The Maps may not look better if viewd in high screen resolution as it has a fixed maximum dimension.
  • The maps may sometimes fail to load on page while searching for places (try clearing the browser's chache and reloading the page)
  • Also, sometimes while toggling between the autocomplete and textsearch, the maps may not give good results.

Future expansion of project:

  • The site can be combined with another APIs to give better results
  • User personalisation can be introduced to save the searches and favourites
  • Personalised search can be introduced
