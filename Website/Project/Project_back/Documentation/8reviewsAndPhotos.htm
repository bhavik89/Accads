User Reviews and photos for place

When the user searches for any place and selects any particular place on the map by clicking on the marker or selecting a place from the list, Place's photos uploaded by google users and Google User reviews are displayed on the page if there are any.

Photos are displayed beneath the maps panel if there are any. User can view the photos by clicking and enlarging them and scrolling through various images.

The place's name is displayed on the top of the reviews box at the bottom right of the page and the reviews are displayed in the box. The user can scroll through user reviews posted by Google Users.

How do we get the photos and reviews:

Photos and Place's reviews are obtained by running the place details service of the Google Maps API. To run this service, it needs the reference ID of the place. The details are obtained in the JSON form and JSON parsed to extract photos and reviews of a place.
